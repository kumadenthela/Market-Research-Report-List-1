<p><strong>The Suspended Solids Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Probe</li><li>Insert Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Suspended Solids Monitor market is divided into two main types: submersible probes and insert probes. Submersible probes are designed to be fully submerged in liquid, providing real-time measurement of suspended solids concentration directly in the fluid. In contrast, insert probes are partially submerged and typically installed through a wall or tank, allowing for easy maintenance while still delivering accurate readings. Both types serve critical roles in wastewater treatment, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes where solid content measurement is essential.</p></p>
