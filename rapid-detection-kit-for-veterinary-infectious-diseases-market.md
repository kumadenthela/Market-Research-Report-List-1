<p><p>The Rapid Detection Kit for Veterinary Infectious Diseases market primarily comprises two types: Antibody Detection and Antigen Detection. Antibody Detection kits identify specific antibodies produced in response to infections, enabling the diagnosis of previous exposure to pathogens. Conversely, Antigen Detection kits target specific antigens from pathogens, allowing for the quick identification of active infections. Both types are crucial for timely diagnosis, vaccination decisions, and overall management of animal health in veterinary practices. Their effectiveness enhances disease control measures in livestock and pets.</p></p>
