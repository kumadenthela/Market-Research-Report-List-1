<p><strong>The Nanosecond Pulsed Laser Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nanosecond Solid Lasers</li><li>Nanosecond Fiber Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanosecond pulsed laser systems market is primarily divided into two types: nanosecond solid lasers and nanosecond fiber lasers. Nanosecond solid lasers utilize solid-state gain media, offering high peak power and versatility for applications like material processing and medical treatments. In contrast, nanosecond fiber lasers leverage optical fibers as gain mediums, providing excellent beam quality and efficiency, while being more compact and easier to manage. Both types are crucial in various industries including manufacturing, telecommunications, and scientific research.</p></p>
