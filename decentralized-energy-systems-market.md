<p><p>Decentralized energy systems encompass various market types, including wind power, hydropower, combined heat and power (CHP) plants, and other thermal power stations. Wind power utilizes turbines to convert wind energy into electricity, while hydropower harnesses flowing or falling water. CHP systems simultaneously generate electricity and useful heat from the same energy source, enhancing efficiency. Other thermal power stations include biomass and geothermal energy facilities. Each market type promotes local energy generation, reduces dependency on centralized grids, and supports sustainability.</p></p>
