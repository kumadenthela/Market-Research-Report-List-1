<p><ul><li>Continuous Type</li><li>Flashed Type</li><li>Pulsed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The panel solar simulator market can be categorized into three main types: Continuous Type, Flashed Type, and Pulsed Type. Continuous Type simulators provide a steady illumination, ideal for long-term testing of solar panels. Flashed Type simulators offer brief, high-intensity light bursts to simulate real solar conditions quickly, useful for efficiency testing. Pulsed Type simulators deliver rapid, short pulses of light, enabling dynamic response analysis of solar cells. Each type serves specific testing needs in solar energy research and production.</p></p>
