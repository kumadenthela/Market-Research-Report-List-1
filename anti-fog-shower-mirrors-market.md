<p><strong>The Anti-Fog Shower Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coated Anti-Fog</li><li>Electrothermal Anti-Fog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-Fog Shower Mirrors market primarily includes two types: coated anti-fog mirrors and electrothermal anti-fog mirrors. Coated anti-fog mirrors utilize special coatings that prevent fogging by reducing surface tension, allowing water to evaporate quickly. In contrast, electrothermal anti-fog mirrors employ a heating element behind the glass to maintain temperature and prevent fog formation. Both types enhance visibility during showers, catering to consumers seeking clearer reflections and improved bathroom experiences, while also finding applications in hotels and spas.</p></p>
