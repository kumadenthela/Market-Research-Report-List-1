<p><p>Precision cancer imaging encompasses various technologies tailored for accurate tumor detection and characterization. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers detailed soft tissue contrast, aiding in the evaluation of complex lesions. Nuclear imaging, including PET scans, reveals metabolic activity in tumors, providing insights into cancer progression. Ultrasound imaging is utilized for real-time visualization and guiding biopsies due to its accessibility and safety. Other imaging modalities encompass advanced techniques like CT scans and optical imaging, enhancing the overall diagnostic landscape in oncology.</p></p>
