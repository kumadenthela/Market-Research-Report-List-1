<p><p>The badminton grip tape market is categorized into three main types: rubber grip, towel grip, and over-grip. Rubber grips provide a firm and durable surface, ideal for players seeking stability and control. Towel grips, made from absorbent materials, are designed for comfort and moisture absorption, making them suitable for extended play. Over-grips are thin layers wrapped over existing grips, enhancing comfort and moisture management while allowing for customization in texture and thickness. Each type caters to different player preferences and performance needs.</p></p>
