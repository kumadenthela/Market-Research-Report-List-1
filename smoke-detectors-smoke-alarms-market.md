<p><p>The smoke detectors and smoke alarms market is primarily divided into two main types: ionization smoke detectors and photoelectric smoke detectors. Ionization detectors are highly sensitive to flaming fires and respond quickly to fast-switching flames, while photoelectric detectors are more effective in detecting smoldering fires with visible smoke. Additionally, there are combined detectors that incorporate both technologies. These devices enhance safety in residential, commercial, and industrial spaces by providing early warnings of smoke and potential fire hazards.</p></p>
