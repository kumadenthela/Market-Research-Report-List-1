<p><p>The textile inks market comprises several types, each tailored for specific applications. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fibers, offering vibrant colors ideal for cotton and cellulosic fabrics. Acidic inks are suited for protein fibers like silk and wool, delivering bright hues through a dyeing process. Paint inks provide a versatile option for various materials. Dispersion inks are used for synthetic fabrics, ensuring high-quality prints, while sublimation inks are designed for transferring images onto polyester surfaces, creating durable and vivid prints.</p></p>
