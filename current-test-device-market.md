<p><strong>The Current Test Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clamp Ammeter</li><li>Probe Ammeter</li><li>Fork Ammeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current test device market comprises three primary types: clamp ammeters, probe ammeters, and fork ammeters. Clamp ammeters are designed to measure current without direct contact by clamping around a conductor, making them safe and easy to use. Probe ammeters utilize probes to make direct electrical contact with circuits for precise readings. Fork ammeters combine features of both, using a fork-like design to measure current via magnetic fields. Each type serves specific applications, catering to varied user needs in electrical diagnostics.</p></p>
