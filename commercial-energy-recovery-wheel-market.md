<p><strong>The Commercial Energy Recovery Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Energy Recovery Wheel market comprises three main types: Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. </p><p>Condensation Rotors primarily focus on recovering energy from exhaust air through phase change by condensing moisture. Hygroscopic Rotors utilize moisture-absorbing materials to exchange humidity between incoming and outgoing air streams, enhancing energy efficiency. Sorption Rotors combine thermal energy transfer with moisture removal, utilizing desiccants to optimize air conditioning processes. Each type enhances HVAC systems by improving energy efficiency and indoor air quality.</p></p>
