<p><ul><li>Analogue Compression Load Cells</li><li>Digital Compression Load Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compression load cells market is primarily divided into two types: analogue and digital compression load cells. Analogue compression load cells use a continuous voltage output that corresponds to the applied load, providing simple integration with existing systems but requiring signal conditioning for improved accuracy. In contrast, digital compression load cells convert the load measurement into a digital signal, allowing for enhanced precision and easier integration with modern data acquisition systems, making them suitable for advanced applications and real-time monitoring.</p></p>
