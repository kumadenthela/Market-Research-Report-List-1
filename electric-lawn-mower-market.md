<p><p>The electric lawn mower market primarily consists of two types: cordless and corded electric mowers. Cordless electric mowers are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering the convenience of mobility without the restrictions of a power cord, making them ideal for larger, uneven lawns. In contrast, corded electric mowers are plugged into an electric outlet, providing continuous power without the need for recharging, making them suitable for smaller, flatter areas. Both types are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional gas mowers, promoting quieter operation and lower emissions.</p></p>
