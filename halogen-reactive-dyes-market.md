<p><p>The Halogen Reactive Dyes market includes two main types: cold brand and hot brand reactive dyes. Cold brand reactive dyes are applied at lower temperatures, allowing for vibrant color fixation on various fabrics without the need for additional heat. In contrast, hot brand reactive dyes require higher temperatures for application, resulting in strong covalent bonds with the textile fibers, often yielding more intense and durable colors. Both types cater to different fabric processing needs and preferences in the textile industry.</p></p>
