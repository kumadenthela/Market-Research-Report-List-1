<p><p>Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) panels are categorized into two main types based on their density and application characteristics. </p><p>Type I MDF is designed for interior applications, featuring a smooth surface and good machinability, making it ideal for furniture, cabinetry, and decorative projects. </p><p>Type II MDF, on the other hand, is engineered for more demanding environments where moisture resistance is required, such as in kitchen and bathroom applications. This type typically offers enhanced durability and is suitable for exterior uses as well.</p></p>
