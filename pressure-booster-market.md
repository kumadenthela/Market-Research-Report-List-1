<p><strong>The Pressure Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Pressure Booster</li><li>Centrifugal Pressure Booster</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure booster market consists of various types designed to enhance fluid pressure in different applications. Volumetric pressure boosters utilize positive displacement mechanisms to increase pressure by drawing in and compressing fluids. Centrifugal pressure boosters, on the other hand, employ rotating impellers to move fluids and create high-pressure outputs, making them ideal for continuous flow systems. Other market types include specialized solutions like diaphragm pumps and ejectors, catering to specific needs across industries such as water supply, HVAC, and manufacturing.</p></p>
