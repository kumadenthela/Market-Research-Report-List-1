<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) Control Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li><li>DP-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering (EPS) control units are categorized into four main types based on their design and functionality. C-EPS (Column EPS) integrates the motor within the column, optimizing space and efficiency. P-EPS (Pinion EPS) allows for direct assistance at the pinion gear, enhancing response. R-EPS (Rack EPS) applies assistance to the steering rack, providing a balanced feel. DP-EPS (Dual Pinion EPS) employs two motors for improved performance and redundancy, ensuring better control and safety for the driver.</p></p>
