<p><ul><li>Nylon Pop Filter</li><li>Metal Pop Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pop filter market features two primary types: nylon and metal pop filters. Nylon pop filters are lightweight and often more affordable, making them popular among beginners and home recording setups. They effectively reduce plosive sounds while being easily replaceable. In contrast, metal pop filters offer durability and a more professional aesthetic, often featuring a dual-layer design to enhance sound quality further. Both types serve the same purpose of minimizing unwanted sounds in vocal recordings, catering to different preferences and budgets.</p></p>
