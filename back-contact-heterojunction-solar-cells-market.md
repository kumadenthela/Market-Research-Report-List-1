<p><p>Back-contact heterojunction solar cells, such as IBC (Interdigitated Back Contact) solar cells, EWT (Elite Wafer Technology) solar cells, and MWT (Metal Wrap Through) solar cells, are characterized by their efficiency and design. IBC cells feature contacts on the back, allowing for better light absorption and less shading. EWT cells enhance efficiency through advanced wafer designs, while MWT cells utilize metal contacts that penetrate the cell. Each type addresses efficiency and performance, making them crucial in the evolving solar energy market.</p></p>
