<p><p>The neurometabolic disorders market includes various conditions characterized by metabolic dysfunctions affecting the nervous system. Gaucher's Disease is caused by glucocerebrosidase enzyme deficiency, leading to organ enlargement and neurological issues. Fabry Disease results from alpha-galactosidase A deficiency, causing pain and skin lesions. Pompe Disease, linked to acid alpha-glucosidase deficiency, affects muscle strength and function. Mucopolysaccharidosis VI involves the accumulation of glycosaminoglycans due to enzyme deficiency, leading to skeletal and neurological complications. The "Other" category encompasses additional rare neurometabolic disorders.</p></p>
