<p><ul><li>Single-Drum Rollers</li><li>Double Drum Rollers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The walk-behind rollers market is primarily categorized into two types: single-drum rollers and double drum rollers. Single-drum rollers feature one large drum, ideal for compacting granular materials in tight spaces, making them suitable for smaller projects. Conversely, double drum rollers have two drums that provide increased stability and even pressure distribution, enhancing efficiency on larger surfaces. Both types are essential for road construction, landscaping, and pavement maintenance, catering to different project requirements and site conditions.</p></p>
