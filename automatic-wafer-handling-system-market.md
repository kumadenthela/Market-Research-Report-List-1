<p><p>The Automatic Wafer Handling System market includes two primary types of boats used for transporting wafers: quartz boats and graphite boats. Quartz boats are preferred for their high thermal stability and resistance to contamination, making them suitable for demanding semiconductor applications. Conversely, graphite boats offer excellent thermal conductivity and are ideal for high-temperature processing environments. Both types play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and reliability of wafer handling in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
