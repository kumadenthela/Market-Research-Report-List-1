<p><strong>The Artificial Turf Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE Artificial Grass Turf</li><li>PP Artificial Grass Turf</li><li>Nylon Artificial Grass Turf</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial turf market is primarily segmented into three types based on the material used: PE (Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), and Nylon. PE artificial grass turf is favored for its realistic appearance and softness, making it suitable for residential lawns and sports fields. PP turf is often used for decorative purposes due to its affordability and color variety, but it is less durable. Nylon turf is the most robust and resilient, making it ideal for high-traffic areas like stadiums, though it is more expensive.</p></p>
