<p><strong>The Ferrite Chokes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chip Ferrite Choke</li><li>Through Hole Ferrite Choke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ferrite Chokes market is primarily divided into two types: Chip Ferrite Chokes and Through Hole Ferrite Chokes. Chip Ferrite Chokes are compact components designed for surface mounting, making them ideal for space-constrained applications, often utilized in modern electronics for effective noise suppression. Through Hole Ferrite Chokes, on the other hand, feature leads that are inserted into holes on a circuit board, offering robust mechanical support and higher current handling capabilities, suitable for larger devices and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
