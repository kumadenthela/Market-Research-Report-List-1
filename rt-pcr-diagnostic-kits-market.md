<p><p>RT PCR diagnostic kits are essential tools for detecting RNA, particularly in virus identification. The market is segmented into one-step and two-step types. One-step kits streamline the process by combining reverse transcription and PCR in a single reaction, enhancing efficiency and minimizing contamination risk. In contrast, two-step kits separate these processes, allowing for greater flexibility and control over the reaction conditions. Both types cater to different laboratory needs, balancing speed and accuracy in diagnostic applications.</p></p>
