<p><p>The bread improver market is categorized into two main types: Universal and Special. Universal type improvers are versatile products suitable for various bread types, enhancing texture, volume, and shelf life regardless of the recipe. They cater to mass production needs. Special type improvers, on the other hand, are tailored for specific bread varieties or unique baking conditions, focusing on niche applications like artisan breads or gluten-free options. This differentiation allows bakers to achieve desired qualities in their final products.</p></p>
