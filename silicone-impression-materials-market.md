<p><strong>The Silicone Impression Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Additional Silicone (A-Silicones)</li><li>Condensation Silicone(C-Silicones)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone impression materials are essential in dentistry and prosthetics, categorized mainly into two types: addition silicones (A-silicones) and condensation silicones (C-silicones). A-silicones offer superior accuracy, stability, and elongation, making them ideal for complex restorations and precise dental applications. C-silicones, while more economical, are less precise and have a shorter working time, which can affect the final impression quality. Both types serve distinct roles in the market, catering to varying needs of dental professionals and technicians.</p></p>
