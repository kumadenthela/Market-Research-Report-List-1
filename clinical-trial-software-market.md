<p><p>The Clinical Trial Software (CTMS) market comprises three primary types: web-based, on-premise, and cloud-based systems. Web-based CTMS offers accessibility from any internet-enabled device, facilitating real-time collaboration among stakeholders. On-premise CTMS involves installing software directly on local servers, giving organizations greater control over their data and security. Cloud-based CTMS provides scalable solutions hosted on external servers, enhancing flexibility and cost-effectiveness while ensuring automatic updates and easier integration with other applications. Each type caters to different organizational needs and environments.</p></p>
