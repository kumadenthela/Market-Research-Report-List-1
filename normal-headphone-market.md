<p><p>The headphone market is divided into three main types: over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear. Over-ear headphones feature large ear cups that fully encompass the ears, providing excellent noise isolation and comfort, ideal for home use. On-ear headphones sit directly on the ears, offering a balance between portability and sound quality, suitable for casual listening. In-ear headphones, or earbuds, fit snugly in the ear canal, making them highly portable and convenient for active lifestyles, often favored by commuters and athletes.</p></p>
