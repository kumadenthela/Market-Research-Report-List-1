<p><p>Power Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) are critical devices that ensure seamless power supply during outages by automatically switching to backup power sources. The market includes several types: Open Transition ATSs, which disconnect the primary power before engaging the backup; Closed Transition ATSs, which allow for a brief overlap between sources for a smoother transition; and Static Transfer Switches (STS), which provide fast switching without mechanical parts. Other types may also exist, catering to various applications and configurations.</p></p>
