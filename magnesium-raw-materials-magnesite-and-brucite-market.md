<p><p>Magnesium raw materials, primarily magnesite and brucite, are essential for various industrial applications. Magnesite comes in two primary forms: phanerocrystalline magnesite, characterized by its large, visible crystalline structure, and cryptocrystalline magnesite, which has a fine-grained texture that is typically not visible to the naked eye. The phanerocrystalline type is favored for its high purity and stability in refractory applications, while cryptocrystalline magnesite is often used in applications requiring finer grain sizes, such as in ceramics and chemical processes.</p></p>
