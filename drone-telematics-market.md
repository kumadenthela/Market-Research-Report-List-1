<p><p>Drone telematics can be categorized into three market types based on range: long range, medium range, and short range. Long-range drones are designed for extensive operations, often exceeding 10 kilometers, suitable for applications like surveillance and delivery. Medium-range drones typically operate between 3 to 10 kilometers, used in agriculture and construction. Short-range drones, generally under 3 kilometers, are ideal for tasks like aerial photography and personal use, focusing on proximity and quick deployment. Each type serves specific operational needs and sectors.</p></p>
