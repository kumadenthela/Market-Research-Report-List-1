<p><p>The instant tea powder market can be categorized into two main types: drinking-use tea and additive-use tea. Drinking-use tea is designed for direct consumption, allowing consumers to easily prepare a convenient beverage. It typically includes flavored or traditional teas that preserve the taste and aroma of brewed tea. Additive-use tea, on the other hand, serves as an ingredient in various food and beverage products, enhancing flavor profiles in items like smoothies, desserts, and baked goods.</p></p>
