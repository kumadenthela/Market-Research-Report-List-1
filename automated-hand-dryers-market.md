<p><p>The automated hand dryers market comprises primarily two types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use high-speed jets of air to quickly evaporate moisture from hands, promoting faster drying times and often employing touchless operation to enhance hygiene. Hot air dryers, on the other hand, blow warm air over wet hands, typically requiring a longer drying duration. Both types are popular in public restrooms and commercial settings, valued for their eco-friendliness and cost-effectiveness compared to paper towels.</p></p>
