<p><p>Sports management software is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on an organization’s servers, offering direct control over data and software customization, but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on third-party servers, allowing for easy access from any device with internet connectivity. This model typically involves subscription pricing, offering scalability and automatic updates, making it a more flexible option for many sports organizations.</p></p>
