<p><p>Shopping mall escalators are categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. Step type escalators are the most common, featuring flat, treaded steps. Belt type escalators utilize a continuous belt for smoother passenger movement. Cleat type escalators have raised cleats for stability when transporting goods. Spiral escalators are designed in a spiral configuration, saving space while enhancing aesthetics. Other types include inclined moving walkways and customized solutions for specific environments, catering to diverse shopping mall needs.</p></p>
